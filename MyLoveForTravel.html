My Love For Travel



My love for travel has existed ever since I have gotten into a car.
My parents have told me that even when I was really young, I have
always loved going to new places and exploring the scenery and meeting
new people. Throughout my life, I have traveled to a variety of places
that have enriched my experience and outlook on the world. During
and after each trip that I have taken, I have a perspective change
as I get to witness new cultures every time I can go somewhere new.
I am forever grateful for the opportunities that I have to travel
and whatever I picked up along the way is sure to stay with me forever.

Los Angeles



One of the most special places that I have traveled to is Los Angeles.
This is a destination that I have traveled far too many times to count
. In this city, there are so many cultures that are crammed into every street,
avenue, block and building and I wish that I could have seen all of them.
However, this goal is far to ambitious and LA will not let me or anyone else
accomplish this as Los Angeles is a ginormous city with culture that just
keeps growing. One of the most notable pieces of LA culture that I have seen
is Street Performance and Break Dancing. Even though LA is the home
of Hollywood and big TV and Movies is royalty in this city, a good street
performance still gives great entertainment and makes your day
that much more vibrant. One of the best Street Performance group that I have enjoyed
is Weezy and Brother from LA. These two brothers perform with heart and most
importantly, they have fun while doing it. During their performances, they have the audience
get involved in the entertainment which makes you feel like you are a part of
the performance. Another great part of LA is the Hollywood and the Hollywood
themed attractions and activities to partake in. I think that you can feel where this is going...

Universal Studios



You might have guessed it. Yep! It is Universal Studios! How could I not talk about Universal Studios when
I mention Los Angeles? I have visited Universal Studios a large number of times ever since I moved
to San Diego. Universal Studios Hollywood offers so many attractions and activities which in my
book, are things that are not okay to miss if and when you pop into Los Angeles. Some of my favorite
things to do in Universal Studios are too first ride the Jurasic Park Ride. This ride
is an icon that represents the heart of the thrill at Universal Studios Hollywood. Since the
LA Branch of Universal Studios is based off of Hollywood, this theme park incorporates all of the
vibrant ideas and thematic elements from all of our favorite American movies. Overall, Universal Studios
is a beacon of entertainment and vibrancy that lights up Los Angeles and this place will always hold a special place in my heart.
